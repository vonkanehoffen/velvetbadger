Festival Info

Welcome to Velvet Badger, the small music festival by a group of friends for more of their friends.
If you're reading this, THIS MEANS YOU!

1 Main Stage, 1 Chillout area, 1 Communal fire + Good friends = Awesome time. To keep up to date with the latest news, please join our facebook page

Camping Rules

We will be having a communal fire so you don't have to start your own. However if you do want your own, fires & BBQ's, they must be off the grass.

Please place any rubbish into bin bags and dispose of them in the recycling bins provided in the main car park. We like to keep to a 'Leave No Trace' policy so please clean up after yourselves.

We need to keep noise to a minimum after 12:30am so please, no boom boxes, howling, bongo battles or any other noisy silliness after this time thank you. We are not putting on a rave so relax your socks and join us by the fire.

Food and Drink

There will be a bar in the main barn serving a selection of Robinsons brewery beverages. You can also bring your own booze but we recommend not to bring glass as glass will not be permitted in the barn. Please drink responsibly.

The Bull I'th' Thorn provides a selection of hot pub grub served during normal hours, view the menu on their website.

On site catering will be provided by .....The Hip-Hop Chip Shop. There's also a Morrison’s supermarket 10 minutes down the road towards Buxton center.

Fancy Dress

Saturday is fancy dress night. The theme will be "SPACE INVASION" so put on your space suits and join our galactic adventure.

Get Involved

If you feel as excited as we do and would like to get involved, get in contact via the favebook page and we'll do our best to make it happen.
