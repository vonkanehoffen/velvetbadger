Info

Welcome to Velvet Badger, the small music festival by a small group of friends for more of their friends. If you've heard of this then it's probably a friend that's told you about it. Or you probably know some of the hand selected acts that we have chosen to play at our little festival. To keep up to date with the latest news, please join our FACEBOOK PAGE

Camping Rules

Fires must be off the grass. We will be having a communal fire so you don't have to start your own. Place any rubbish into bin bags and dispose of them in the recycling bins provided in the main car park. We like to keep to a 'Leave No Trace' policy so please clean up after yourselves. Noise levels must be kept to a minimum after 12:30am so please, no boom boxes, howling, bongo battles or any other noisy silliness after this time thank you. We are not putting on a rave so relax your socks and join us by the fire.

Food and Drink

There will be a bar in the main barn serving a selection of Robinsons brewery beverages. You can also bring your own booze but we recommend not to bring glass as glass will not be permitted in the barn. The Bull I'th' Thorn provides a selection of hot pub grub served during normal hours, view the menu on their website here. On site catering will be provided by The Hip-Hop Chip Shop. There's also a Morrison’s supermarket 10 minutes down the road towards Buxton center.
